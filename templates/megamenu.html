    • How it Works

    Your story matters. Without it, nothing happens. Floodlight is a tool for creating meaningful stories on the web that are visually compelling, supported by facts and encourage community action. Floodlight is also a place to share stories and uncover connections between them.

    On Floodlight, you can…

    Build a breakthrough story using photos, videos, data and more.
    Learn how to be a better storyteller through training and resources.
    Read and contribute to the stories others are sharing.

    By connecting storytellers, story sharers and story readers, Floodlight is a project to brighten, inform and transform community though the power of story.
